**File Viewing Commands**
### **cat**
- Show full file content (no scroll back).
- Can concatenate multiple files.
### **tac**
- Like `cat`, but prints from bottom to top.
### **less**
- Best pager for large files.
- Supports scrolling, jumping, searching.
- Search:
    - `/pattern` — search forward
    - `?pattern` — search backward
### **head**
- Show the first 10 lines (default).
- `head -n 15 file` — first 15 lines.
### **tail**
- Show the last 10 lines (default).
- `tail -n 20 file` — last 20 lines.
