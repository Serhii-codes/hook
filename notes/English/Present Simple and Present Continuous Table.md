| State verbs                                 | Used in Simple because they describe a state, not an action: - Feeling / wanting: like, love, hate, prefer, need, want, wish.- Thinking / believing: believe, know, remember, think, understand.- Being / appearing: be, seem, appear.- Possession: have (got), own, possess.- Senses: hear, see, smell, taste. | I know the answer.This cake tastes delicious.                                                                                                                                                     | Not used in Continuous (except when meaning changes).                                                                                                                                                                               | ❌ I am knowing the answer.✔ I’m seeing the doctor tomorrow.                                                                     |
