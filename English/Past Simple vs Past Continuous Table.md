
| Category                             | Past Simple                                                                 | Examples (Past Simple)                                  | Past Continuous                                                                                 | Examples (Past Continuous)                                                           |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Main meaning                         | Completed actions in the past                                               | I visited Paris last year. She cooked dinner yesterday. | Actions in progress at a specific moment in the past.                                           | I was reading at 8 pm yesterday. They were playing football when it started to rain. |
|                                      | Actions one after another.                                                  | He opened the door, walked in, and sat down.            | Actions at the same time                                                                        | I was watching TV while my brother was reading.                                      |
|                                      | Permanent                                                                   | He worked as a teacher all his life.                    | Temporary                                                                                       | I was staying at my friend’s house that week.                                        |
| Interruptions (short vs long action) | The short action that interrupts another action.                            | The phone rang while she was cooking.                   | The long action that was interrupted by something else.                                         | She was cooking when the phone rang.                                                 |
|                                      | Past habits                                                                 | I always walked to school as a child.                   | Past annoying habits                                                                            | He was always losing his keys!                                                       |
|                                      |                                                                             |                                                         | Background for another action.                                                                  | It was raining when I left home.                                                     |
| Duration / Repetition                | Short or repeated actions, not focusing on duration.                        | He called me twice yesterday.                           | Longer actions in progress, showing duration.                                                   | She was studying all evening.                                                        |
| Temporary situations / Background    | Focus on finished event.                                                    | I lived in London for two years.                        | Ongoing situation in the past.                                                                  | I was living in London when I met her.                                               |
|                                      |                                                                             |                                                         | Plans that didn’t happen                                                                        | I was planning to go to the party, but I got sick.                                   |
| Signal words                         | yesterday, last week/month/year, ago, in 2010, then, after, suddenly, when. | We met two days ago.                                    | while, when, all evening, all day, yesterday at 5 pm (specific moment or duration in the past). | I was sleeping when he came. They were watching TV at 7 pm yesterday.                |
| Formation rule                       | +: Subject + V2/ed -: Subject + did not + V1 ?: Did + subject + V1          | She worked in a bank. Did he play football?             | +: Subject + was/were + V-ing -: Subject + was/were + not + V-ing ?: Was/Were + subject + V-ing | He was reading a book. Were you watching TV at 6 pm?                                 |
