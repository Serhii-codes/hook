# üéØ **Important Rules**

### ‚úîÔ∏è **1. Position of adverbs**

Adverbs can be placed:
- **front position**: _Usually, I wake up early._
- **mid position**: _I usually wake up early._
- **end position**: _I wake up early usually._ (less common, but possible)

### ‚úîÔ∏è **2. Don‚Äôt confuse adjective vs adverb**
- She is **quick** (adjective ‚Üí describes a noun).
- She runs **quickly** (adverb ‚Üí describes a verb).

### ‚úîÔ∏è **3. Adverbs NOT ending in -ly**
- fast
- hard
- late
- early
- well
### ‚úîÔ∏è **4. Adverbs modifying adjectives or other adverbs**
- **very** big (adjective)
- **extremely** fast (adverb)
